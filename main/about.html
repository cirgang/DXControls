Smaller Systems.
Greater Autonomy.

Our philosophy is straightforward: engineer reliable, cost-efficient, American-made robotic systems with modularity built in from the start.

Our Vision

We see a future of smaller, smarter robotic systems working together. A coordinated network of compact UAVs could allow one operator to supervise an entire fleet. Modular, low-cost platforms can support missions ranging from security to search and rescue.

We develop miniature UAVs, embedded controllers, and autonomous sensing platforms in close collaboration with customers. That partnership keeps each system practical, reliable, and aligned with its mission.

Our Approach

01
Foundation

Embedded Systems

Purpose-built computers and control hardware form the foundation of every DXC platform. Our UAV experience helps us design compact electronics around the constraints of flight rather than adapting generic hardware afterward.

02
Intelligence

Autonomous Robotics

Sensors, intelligent control, and machine-learning methods build on that hardware foundation, allowing robotic platforms to perceive their surroundings and act without constant operator input.

03
Coordination

Mission Software

Reliable software turns individual vehicles into useful systems. DXC focuses on rapid iteration, secure communication, and networked coordination between multiple robotic platforms.

04
Adaptability

Modularity

Interchangeable cameras, sensing packages, compute modules, and payloads let customers configure a common platform for distinct missions without rebuilding the aircraft around each use case.
