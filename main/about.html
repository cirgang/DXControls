The Next Generation of Robotics

DXControls develops autonomous robotic platforms integrated with AI for commercial, security, and advanced research applications.


Our philosophy is simple: engineer reliable, cost-efficient, and American-made robotics with multi-purpose modularity.

Our Vision

Here at DXC, we see a future with smaller, smarter, and more capable robotics that work together. A network of UAVs which cooperate, analogous to a swarm of bees, would require only a single operator. From security to rescue, high modularity with low-cost of these robotics systems offers use across a wide-array of industries.

Whether developing miniature UAVs, custom embedded controllers, or autonomous sensing platforms, our engineering-first mindset requires strong customer-producer relationships. With great ambitions comes great responsibility to deliver reliable, high-quality products.

Our Approach

Embedded Systems

Embedded systems are the computers/hardware designed for a specific task. DXC specialists have unique and extensive history with UAV embedded systems, which enables realization of our vision.

Autonomous Robotics

Designing autonomous robotics relies upon intelligent control systems: the foundation of AI. Building upon strong hardware, various machine learning techniques and algorithmic techniques enable novel autonomy, whereby products can act without constant human operation.

Innovative Software

Rapid iteration, practical engineering, and continuous improvement for autonomous robotics creates distinct challenges, which requires powerful software. Alongside AI, our engineers focus upon meshed networks to enable communication between various robotic systems.

Modularity

With developed platforms, such as UAV systems, modularity is vital to our vision. Whether installing a certain camera system, intelligence unit, or payloads for various tasks, our hardware enables customers to find individualized uses for our platforms.
